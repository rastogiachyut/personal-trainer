### Day A — "Squat/Press + Intensity Techniques"

#### 🔥 RAMP Warm-Up (10 min)
1. **Raise:** 5 min brisk walk or treadmill
2. **Mobilise:** Arm circles 10×, torso twists 10×, leg swings 10/side
3. **Activate:** Glute bridges 15 reps, bird-dog 8/side
4. **Potentiate:** 2 ramp-up sets of goblet squat at 50% → 75% target weight

#### 💪 Main Workout

**A1. Goblet Squat** — *Cluster Sets*
| Set | Format | Weight | Rest |
|-----|--------|--------|------|
| 1 | 5 reps → 15s pause → 5 reps → 15s pause → 5 reps | 12-15 kg DB | 2.5 min after cluster |
| 2 | Same cluster format | Same or +2.5 kg | 2.5 min |
| 3 | Same cluster format | Same weight | — |

*Technique:* 3-second eccentric (lower slowly), 1-second pause at bottom, explode up. After hitting failure on full reps, do **3-5 lengthened partials** (partial squats in the bottom stretched position).

**A2. DB Bicep Curls** — *Myo-rep Format*
| Set | Format | Weight |
|-----|--------|--------|
| Activation | 10-12 reps to near failure | 10 kg/hand |
| Mini 1 | 3-5 reps | Same |
| Mini 2 | 3-5 reps | Same |
| Mini 3 | 3-5 reps | Same |

*Rest between mini-sets:* 15 seconds only. *Technique:* 2-sec eccentric, squeeze at top.

**B1. Deadlift** — *Standard with Tempo*
| Set | Reps | Weight | Tempo | Rest |
|-----|------|--------|-------|------|
| 1 | 8-10 | Rod + 5-10 kg plates | 3-1-1-0 | 2.5 min |
| 2 | 8-10 | Same or +5 kg | 3-1-1-0 | 2.5 min |
| 3 | 8-10 | Same weight | 3-1-1-0 | — |

*Technique:* Brace hard (Valsalva), slow pull from floor, controlled lowering (3 sec).

**B2. Chest Fly Machine + Push-up** — *Post-Exhaustion Superset*
| Set | Exercise A | Exercise B | Rest |
|-----|-----------|-----------|------|
| 1 | Chest Fly 10 reps | Push-up max reps (incline if needed) | 90s |
| 2 | Chest Fly 10 reps | Push-up max reps | 90s |
| 3 | Chest Fly 10 reps | Push-up max reps | — |

*Technique:* On chest fly, focus on stretch at bottom (lengthened position). Squeeze chest hard at top (mind-muscle connection).

**C1. DB Shoulder Press** — *Standard + Lengthened Partials*
| Set | Reps | Weight | Rest |
|-----|------|--------|------|
| 1 | 10 | 7.5-10 kg/hand | 90s |
| 2 | 10 | Same or +1.25 kg | 90s |
| 3 | 10 + 3-5 partials at bottom | Same | — |

*Technique:* Back against pad for support. Lower to shoulder level (full stretch). After failure, do partial reps in the bottom half of the movement.

**C2. Tricep Pushdown** — *Myo-rep Format*
| Set | Format | Weight |
|-----|--------|--------|
| Activation | 12 reps to near failure | 25-30 kg |
| Mini 1 | 3-5 reps | Same |
| Mini 2 | 3-5 reps | Same |
| Mini 3 | 3-5 reps | Same |

*Rest between mini-sets:* 15 seconds. *Technique:* Slow eccentric (2 sec), full extension at bottom, squeeze tricep hard.

#### 🧊 Cool-Down (5 min)
- 3 min easy walk
- Foam roll or stretch: quads (30s/side), chest (30s), lats (30s/side), hamstrings (30s)
- Static stretching only post-workout

### Day B — "Pull/Hinge + Intensity Techniques"

#### 🔥 RAMP Warm-Up (10 min)
Same structure — Raise (treadmill), Mobilise (arm circles, hip openers), Activate (glute bridges, band pull-aparts if available), Potentiate (2 ramp-up sets of lat pulldown at 50% → 75%).

#### 💪 Main Workout

**A1. Romanian Deadlift (RDL)** — *Tempo + Lengthened Partials*
| Set | Reps | Weight | Tempo | Rest |
|-----|------|--------|-------|------|
| 1 | 8-10 | Rod + light plates | 3-0-1-0 | 2.5 min |
| 2 | 8-10 | Same or +5 kg | 3-0-1-0 | 2.5 min |
| 3 | 8 + 5 partials at bottom stretch | Same | 3-0-1-0 | — |

*Key:* This is a HINGE not a squat. Push hips back, feel hamstring stretch, drive hips forward. The partials at the bottom work the hamstring at its longest length — most hypertrophy per rep.

**A2. Lat Pulldown** — *Mind-Muscle Connection Focus*
| Set | Reps | Weight | Rest |
|-----|------|--------|------|
| 1 | 10 | 25-30 kg | 2 min |
| 2 | 10 | 30-35 kg | 2 min |
| 3 | 10 + 3-5 partials at top stretch | Same | — |

*Technique:* Think "elbows to ribs" not "hands to chest." Squeeze shoulder blades together at bottom. Control the eccentric (2-3 sec). Partial reps = let the bar go back up to the stretched position (arms fully extended) and do half-pulls from there.

**B1. Seated Cable Row + DB One-Arm Row** — *Post-Exhaustion Superset*
| Set | Exercise A | Exercise B | Rest |
|-----|-----------|-----------|------|
| 1 | Cable Row 10 reps | DB Row 8-10/side | 90s |
| 2 | Cable Row 10 reps | DB Row 8-10/side | 90s |
| 3 | Cable Row 10 reps | DB Row 8-10/side | — |

*Weight:* Cable row 25-35 kg, DB row 10-12.5 kg. *Technique:* Squeeze lat for 1 sec at peak of every rep.

**B2. Goblet Squat or Leg Press** — *Cluster Sets*
| Set | Format | Weight | Rest |
|-----|--------|--------|------|
| 1 | 5 → 15s → 5 → 15s → 5 | 12 kg DB / moderate machine | 2.5 min |
| 2 | Same cluster | Same | 2.5 min |
| 3 | Same cluster | Same | — |

*Technique:* Deep as comfortably possible. Pause 1 sec at bottom for goblet squat.

**C1. EZ Bar or DB Curl** — *Myo-rep Format*
| Set | Format | Weight |
|-----|--------|--------|
| Activation | 10-12 reps | 10 kg/hand or EZ bar |
| Mini 1-3 | 3-5 reps × 3, 15s rest | Same |

**C2. Overhead Tricep Extension** — *Lengthened Partials*
| Set | Reps | Weight | Rest |
|-----|------|--------|------|
| 1 | 12 | Heavy DB, both hands | 60s |
| 2 | 12 | Same | 60s |
| 3 | 12 + 5 partials at bottom stretch | Same | — |

### 📐 Progression System (Optimized)

**For compound lifts (squat, deadlift, RDL, lat pulldown, cable row):**
- **Week 1:** Find your 10-rep weight
- **Week 2-3:** Add 1 rep per session until all sets hit 10
- **Week 4:** Add 2.5 kg (lower body) or 1.25 kg (upper body), drop to 8 reps
- **Repeat cycle**
- **Every 4th week:** Deload (2 sets instead of 3, same weight)

**For isolation lifts (curls, pushdowns, lateral raises):**
- **Myo-rep format:** Activation set of 10-12, then 3 mini-sets of 3-5 reps with 15s rest
- **When activation set hits 15 reps:** Increase weight by 1.25 kg
- **Lengthened partials:** Always add 3-5 partial reps at the stretched position after hitting failure on the last set

**For bodyweight (push-ups, planks):**
- Add 1 rep per session
- When push-ups hit 15 clean reps, progress to harder variation

### 🎯 What to Track (Optimized)

**Per session:**
- Exercise, weight, reps for every set
- Note which intensity techniques were used (myo-rep, cluster, partials)
- Rate of Perceived Exertion (RPE 1-10) for the session
- Any pain or discomfort

**Weekly (Sunday):**
- Body weight (morning, post-bathroom)
- Waist measurement (navel)
- How clothes fit (looser/tighter)
- Gym performance trend (going up = muscle growing)

**Monthly:**
- Photos (front, side, back — same lighting)
- Arm/thigh measurements
- Strength audit (heaviest weights used this month vs last)
